I'm Alejandro
Graphic Designer. Front End Developer. Photograper. Friend. Bmx Rider. Gamer.

As a graphic designer I spent almost 10 years crafting and creating different designs for several clients. From there, I dug my fork into competetive eating, but turns out I bit off far more than I could chew. So the next logical step was jumping into front end web devolopment.
